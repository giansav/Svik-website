About Me

Welcome to Svikò Jewelry! I'm Svitlana Kochetova, an artist and jewelry designer with a passion for transforming creativity into wearable art. Since 2015, I have been working with the intricate technique of beadwork, designing and crafting each piece entirely by hand.

Every creation is born from my vision, shaped with meticulous attention to detail, and infused with passion. My goal is to celebrate individuality by offering unique, custom-made jewelry that tells your story. Each piece is more than just an accessory—it’s a reflection of your essence, a treasure made just for you.
